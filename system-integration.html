System Integration


Every enterprise faces major challenges such as adopting complex technologies, ever changing business environments and reducing cost, integrating multiple systems that cater to different functions, departments and stages in product lifecycles.

iQuent's global experience and expertise in Systems Integration can help enterprises to ensure that the technology is strategically aligned to meet the business objectives regardless of the size and nature of the organization.

We deliver cost-effective integration services that are steady, scalable, effective, efficient and innovative.

iQuent, as an experienced Systems Integration partner, helps enterprises realize business benefits quickly and improve ROI, while leveraging industry best practices.

Key Services we offer:

  • OSS System Integration
  • BSS System Integration
  • Network Consulting and Integration
  • Enterprise Application Integration
  • Service Management Platform Integration

OSS and BSS System Integration

OSS System Integration

Our OSS System Integration Services provide design and integration services for OSS systems used for supervision, configuration, deployment and optimization of Networks.

We have experienced OSS consultants with years of experience in integrating OSS systems for Tier-1 operators.

BSS System Integration

Our BSS System Integration Services span the entire lifecycle from consulting, requirement analysis, design and architecture to customization, solution integration, testing, deployment and migration.

Our extensive Systems Integration experience in Billing and Mediation systems help operators deploy convergent BSS platforms quickly or transform legacy billing systems to convergent BSS platforms.

Our OSS and BSS System Integration Services cover:

  • Integration of OSS and BSS systems / applications
  • Service Management Platform Integration
  • Integration of data
  • Integration of process, activities, knowledge and information

iQuent's value add:

  • Faster network or product rollout
  • Improved revenue margins
  • Improved customer experience
  • Simplified, consolidated and streamlined systems, processes and tools
  • Improved efficiency
  • Efficient data management
  • Lower operating costs to drive profitability

Network Consulting and Integration

Our Network Consulting and Integration Services provide services covering the complete lifecycle of Carrier and Enterprise networks from network design, build, optimization to network operations for global fixed and wireless communication providers.

We follow a life cycle approach to consulting and integration services which are flexible and are specifically designed to meet network connectivity needs. Our experienced professionals apply proven methodologies to help develop an end-to-end network solution for customer's business and IT requirements.

We have developed over time a repository of proven methods, knowledge, ideas, tools and best practices to help plan, implement and monitor customer's network and infrastructure.

Our Network Consulting and Integration Services help operators to:

  • Minimize network capacity incidents
  • Simplify performance management and optimize processes
  • Reduce operational cost
  • Improve customer satisfaction

Enterprise Application Integration

Our Enterprise Application Integration Services offer the complete lifecycle of Enterprise Application Management from development, integration, testing, to deployment, hosting and support. Our team of experts will help you achieve seamless business process and data integration for your organization.

Our Enterprise Application Integration Services have been derived from years of cross-domain experience and technology expertise.

  • Enabling Collaboration within the organization

  • Enabling smooth integration of applications and data
