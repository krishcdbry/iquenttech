Enterprise Applications

Enterprise Application Management


iQuent specializes in providing customized Application Development, Testing and Maintenance Services to its clients worldwide. iQuent rely on industry-standard software development practices and rigorously test the applications using industry’s best test practices. Our deep domain expertise allows us to develop scalable and flexible enterprise applications, providing our customers next-generation applications generating greater efficiency.

Our Application Development model is an outcome of various innovations and enhancements, and our framework is designed to deliver products that not only fulfils clients’ requirements, but also adheres to global software development standards. Our vast experience in Application development has helped us come out with such generic framework, which support diverse platforms. Quality planning and design is the key to our successful delivery.

iQuent has long experience of developing integrated enterprise applications for Web as well as for Mobile. We develop mobile applications for the following type of mobiles/ platforms.

  • Android
  • iOS
  • Windows
  • BlackBerry
  • Symbian

Mobile Applications Development

iQuent has vast experience of successfully building Mobile Applications and bringing the best and brightest software ideas to life. Our expertise in mobile applications development enables us to provide quick, high quality and cost effective solutions to companies around the world.

With iQuent you can leverages cost and scalability advantage to significantly reduce development cost across various mobile Application Developments.

iQuent's Area of Expertise (AoE)

iPhone Apps Development

iQuent has expertise in developing iOS application for enterprises. Our expert team approaches development in a planned way. We coordinate with our client right from day one.

Android Apps Development

iQuent has expertise in developing Android Application and Custom based Mobile Applications. We develop third party applications in Java which run on Android Platform. We have a proficient expertise in creating android applications, testing with simulator, and porting to mobile devices.

Blackberry Application Development

iQuent has skilled team to develop BlackBerry applications as well. We have expertise in Blackberry Application Development (Both Middle & CLDC Application), Blackberry software development and Blackberry Programming. iQuent works closely with clients to understand business needs and develop:

  • Client/Server Application
  • Wireless Solutions
  • Internet based Solutions
  • Migration of applications from J2ME to Blackberry and vice versa

Windows Mobile Application Development

iQuent offers all kinds of custom development for Windows Mobile Platform. iQuent works closely with clients to understand business needs and develop:

  • Embedded Software Development for Smartphone and Pocket PC
  • Application Development for Wireless data Transfers
  • Data Synchronization
  • Client Applications which include offline and online functionality
