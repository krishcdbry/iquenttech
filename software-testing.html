Software Testing


The spread of networks and mobility has brought an increase in devices and platforms. This gives a considerable importance to testing as enterprises look at time-to-market factor and optimise costs while adhering to latest ISO standards.

We provide end-to-end service including Test Strategy, Test Planning, Test Management, Test Automation and Test Execution. We help enterprises deliver high quality products on-time and within budget.

iQuent provides a comprehensive range of testing services from compatibility testing to functional testing to in-depth feature conformance testing including:

Test Infrastructure

iQuent has all the necessary infrastructure to test both in lab environments and live networks. We provide infrastructure for hosting all phases of testing. Our infrastructure services can be used to host enterprise mobility management systems and systems supporting enterprise mobile applications

Testing

Our out-of-box test strategy combines different testing techniques and methods that together provide the best overall testing results thereby balancing the trade-offs between cost, quality and time to market. Our holistic approach helps enterprises through all phases of testing lifecycle such as

  • Functional Testing
  • Non-functional Testing
  • Unit Testing
  • System Integration Testing
  • Regression Testing
  • Load and Performance Testing
  • User Acceptance Testing

Test Automation

Our automated test approach is flexible and robust which provides cost and time effectiveness for multiple release cycles and helps in reducing the time-to-market for any release.

Test Management

Our test management approach uses industry standard test management products such as HP QualityCenter and IBM Rational Quality Manager that enables us to manage test planning and execution efficiently.

  • Improved Test Management and Reporting
  • Improved Product Quality
  • Cost Optimization
  • Time to Market advantage
  • Improved Risk Management
  • Strengthened Competitive Position
