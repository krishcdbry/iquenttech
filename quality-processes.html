Quality Management

Quality Management


iQuent has a well-defined software development process and quality management system. Our philosophy is to make process work for us than to work for the processes. Therefore, our QMS allows us to tailor SDLC to suit varied customer requirements based business need. We do development based on water fall model, iterative model, adapting iterative model and as well as latest Agile model.

Quality Process

iQuent follows SDLC from requirement analysis to deployment support. We have very well structured and managed requirements definition process.

We recommend and use tools based on business need and costs. Our philosophy is that desired quality and high productivity can be achieved with the right mix of tools and processes. We evaluate cautiously and continuously the open source to keep cost under check.

We make maximum reuse of the software component and processes to give advantage to our customers and focus more on auto code generators to enhance productivity.

We understand that requirements for Telecom technologies are evolving and change is the only constant therefore our processes have be made to manage the changes in scope from beginning and we have very well defined change management system in place.

Following our stringent Quality Approach, we achieve:

  • Strengthened competitive position
  • Higher productivity
  • Improved customer focus and satisfaction
  • Reduced costs and higher profitability
  • Improved and innovative processes
  • Adaptability to changing or emerging market conditions
