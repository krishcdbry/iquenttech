Enterprise Mobility Management


The shift to mobile is dramatically re-shaping the way people work and organisation do business. Businesses are embracing mobility as a strategy to deliver real, measurable and sustainable improvement in business performance. IT departments are being continuously challenged to effectively deliver and manage solutions to support mobile enterprise.

Key challenges faced by enterprises are:

Management:

Mobile Device Management: Mobile devices are quickly becoming the primary communications and computing platform for business. This presents a new amazing opportunity for businesses to make their employees more productive on devices that they love to use. However, the adoption of smartphones and tablets in the enterprise introduce cost, security risk, and usability challenges that traditional IT management tools cannot address.

Mobile Application Management: Enterprise mobility requires end-user mobile applications to access enterprise content and systems. Distribution and management of mobile applications is an important task in achieving secure and scalable model for enterprise mobility.

Security:

With mobility comes the increased threat to the enterprise as it loses the control over the actual hardware and the possibility of losing it becomes high. Therefore securing the enterprise content on the mobile devices has become priority for an organisation.

Visibility and Control:

An enterprise would like to have some level of control over the mobile devices that are used to access enterprise resources. The control over who can access the data and how to handle a compromised device are important for an enterprise to ensure smooth functioning.
