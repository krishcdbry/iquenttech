Testing and Validation

Everyone expects to connect to businesses anytime, anywhere. Product and application quality and performance is critical in achieving success in today's connected world.

This has increased the focus on quality, and hence every enterprise is improving its testing practices to adapt to the changing demands from digital transformation. They mainly focus on tangible business outcomes and are very cautious about its investments. They always look for new ways to improve quality & speed and reduce cost while managing this digital transformation.

iQuent thinks differently and believes in adopting industrialized processes and infusing intelligence into delivery through its expertise, predictive analytics and automation making it easy for customers to identify areas for improved business value and quantify the expected returns with accelerated results.

We provide end-to-end testing services including Integration Testing, System Testing, User Acceptance Testing, Compatibility Testing, Load and Performance Testing and Security Testing for wide range of products and services.

Our Test Strategy and Approach mainly focuses on:

  • Reducing Time-to-Market
  • Reducing testing costs, efforts and resources
  • Enhancing the level of Quality
  • Automation and increasing reusability of Testing assets
