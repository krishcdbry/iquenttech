Business Process / Workflow Automation


Having a robust and scalable business automation system can significantly increase the efficiency of business by reducing overhead costs, increasing the speed and quality of services / operations, increasing capacity, and ultimately improving profitability.

iQuent helps Tier 1 companies and Govt sector organizations offering them cost effective complete business automation systems to help them focus on their core business by implementing robust systems customized as per their business needs including Human Resource Management, Finance, Procurement, Quality and Sales.

iQuent’s benchmarked practices and processes ensure that systems are easy and pleasant to use, delivered on time and within budget and there is no impact to our customers business during transition.

As preferred Business Automation Company, we have delivered significant benefits to our global customers. Our custom business automation process encompasses all the phases of software development life cycle, starting from translating business needs into project requirements through implementation followed by support.

iQuent offers business automation services enhancing client value at every stage in the business lifecycle from an initial idea, through implementation to transition to new business solution including:

  • Business Consulting: Understanding existing processes, identifying area of improvement, advising business options etc.
  • Project Management: Ongoing project management, change control and configuration management are key activities ensuring successful business transformation
  • System design including High Level, Low Level design
  • Software development as per customer’s business requirements
  • Software implementation including data migration ensuring smooth transition
  • Maintenance & 24x7 support
