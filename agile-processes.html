Agile Development

Agile Development Process


Time to market is a very big challenge in today's business scenario. Instead of waiting for months for the final product to come out, customers want to have better predictability on overall product delivery.

To help our customers meet this challenge, we follow Agile development process where software is developed in incremental, rapid cycles. This results in small incremental releases with each release building on previous functionality. Each release is thoroughly tested to ensure software quality is maintained.

Agile process can afford more flexibility compared to traditional waterfall model, which lock in the project details early and are less able to adapt to customers evolving needs.

Agile approach at iQuent helps to achieve:

  • Improved Customer Satisfaction
  • Improved Stakeholder Engagement
  • Fast and Predictable Delivery
  • Improved Quality
  • Improved Predictability on Cost and Schedule
  • Streamlined Process Overhead
  • Better Risk Management
